Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

Step into the magical world of Harry Potter in this enchanting tale where an orphaned boy discovers he is a wizard on his 11th birthday. Harry embarks on an extraordinary journey to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he uncovers the truth about his past, makes lifelong friends, and encounters the dark wizard Voldemort, who killed his parents. Packed with mystery, magic, and heartwarming friendships, the movie sets the stage for the epic saga of bravery and adventure.
